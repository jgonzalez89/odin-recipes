Guac Recipe

Description

This is the guacamole recipe as it’s simple to make and uses fresh, high-quality ingredients. Authentic guacamole doesn’t contain fillers and unnecessary ingredients. All you need is avocados, onion, tomatoes, cilantro, jalapeno pepper, lime juice, garlic, and salt. Easy and delicious!

Ingredients

  • Cilantro
  • Red Onion
  • Avocado
  • Lime

Steps

  1. Slice three ripe avocados in half, remove the pit and scoop them into a mixing bowl.
  2. Then use a fork to gently mash them to your desired level of chunky or smooth.
  3. Add the onions, tomatoes, cilantro, jalapeno pepper, garlic, lime juice and salt and stir everything together.
